Amazon

Amazon replaced its best search results with the highest bidder and called it shopping.

You searched for the best product. You thought you were seeing the best product. You were seeing whoever paid the most to appear first. Amazon's own employees called their search results "a river of junk." The organic results — the honest ones — got pushed down. Then further down. Then off the page entirely. Convenience became a con.

Source: FTC v. Amazon, 2023. Internal Amazon documents entered into evidence.

We find the best one. Period.
